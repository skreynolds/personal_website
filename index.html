Teaching

Nexus EdX
I love teaching. I was a bit sad when I hung up my High School teaching hat so I could follow my other passions (e.g. robots). I run this site, Nexus EdX, which gives me some capacity to get my chalk and talk happening once more. The platform runs on the open source code which powers EdX, and offers a selection of Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics subjects. The course content is made up of short 10 minute videos, which are punctuated by exercises to help learners solidify their understanding. The cost to use the site is $40 per subject per semester, but if you want to view the videos for free, I have them listed on YouTube.
